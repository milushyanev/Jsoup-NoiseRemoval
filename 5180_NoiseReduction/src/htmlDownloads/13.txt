       <h2 class="featurette-heading">Welcome, Parents &amp; Families <br><span class="text-muted"></span> </h2> 
       <p class="lead"> </p>
       <p><span><span>The Parent and Family team speaks for everyone at the university when we say we're honored you and your student have chosen or are considering Cal Poly Pomona. Parents and family members are a crucial partner in the success of our students. There are many ways to get involved -- please sign up for the&nbsp;</span><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/family/volunteer.shtml"><span>parent and family newsletter</span></a> to stay informed on important information and download the <a href="https://www.docdroid.net/RQ6dyDg/cpp-parent-and-family-resource-guide-pdf">Parent and Family Resource Guide</a> to familiarize yourself with CPP.</span></p> 
       <p><span>COVID-19 has had an enormous impact on our university and our nation. As a result, we'd like to take a moment to recognize your patience and understanding as we navigate a new reality, together.&nbsp;Ensuring our students have adequate resources to succeed in the virtual environment remains our top priority, whether it be <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/zoom-resources.shtml" title="https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/zoom-resources.shtml">Zoom and online learning resources</a>, information about <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/accessing-services.shtml" title="https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/accessing-services.shtml">academic advising</a>&nbsp;<a href="https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/accessing-services.shtml" title="https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/accessing-services.shtml"></a>or staying <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/student-affairs/covid19.shtml">up-to-date</a> on the many ways students can remain supported as an active member of their community in an online setting.</span></p> 
       <p><span><span>We encourage you to reach out with any questions, concerns or comments using the contact methods listed below. Go Broncos!</span></span></p>
       <p></p>
