      <h2>Our Commitment</h2> 
      <p><strong>Fall is predominantly virtual.</strong> We are operating under virtual instruction, with very limited exceptions for courses with an in-person component.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>Health and safety are high priority.</strong> To mitigate the risk and spread of COVID-19, only faculty, staff and students with approved in-person instruction or other activities should come to campus. Virtual training and a daily health screener are required.</span></p> 
      <p><span><strong>Supporting Student Success. </strong>We want all students to have a positive experience and fully engage in virtual/remote education. This website links student resources for academic support, student life, health and wellness and more.</span></p> 
      <p><span>(Page updated 9/15/2020)</span></p> 
     </section>

      <h2>Phased Reopening Considerations</h2> 
      <p>In all our planning efforts, we are guided by our commitment to protect health and well-being, and provide an educational experience that lives up to our standards and legacy as an inclusive polytechnic university. We will continue to follow the guidelines and recommendations from the CSU Chancellor's Office, state and local public health agencies, and protocols for higher education institutions and office-based work sites outlined in <a href="https://nam03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fpublichealth.lacounty.gov%2Fmedia%2FCoronavirus%2Fdocs%2FHOO%2FHOO_COVID-19_Safer%2520at%2520Work%2520and%2520in%2520the%2520Community-Phase%25203_bars%2520closed_06282020.pdf&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cestherchou%40cpp.edu%7C740392314481415e654708d82a8d9575%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637306135089940782&amp;sdata=wCFjK%2B8SfGK9hbeI1RnEzVL8T%2FLBgHcgTQJ6DTwG%2BRY%3D&amp;reserved=0">Reopening Safer at Work and the Community for Control of COVID-19 Order</a>.</p>
