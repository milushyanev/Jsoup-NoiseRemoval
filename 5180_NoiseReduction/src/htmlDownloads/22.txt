     <h1 class="sr-only">About Cal Poly Pomona</h1> 
     <section class="text-images" id="s0">
       Cal Poly Pomona is among the best public universities in the West and is nationally ranked for helping students achieve economic success. As an inclusive polytechnic university, we cultivate success through experiential learning, discovery and innovation. Our graduates are ready to succeed in the professional world on Day 1. 
      <p>Faculty in all disciplines put theory to practice, providing students with opportunities to apply their knowledge in hands-on projects, research collaborations, and valuable internship and service-learning programs.</p> 
      <p>Our history and geography are unlike any other university in the region. Nowhere else can students ride an Arabian horse, practice on a Steinway piano, bring a new product to market, and build a liquid-fueled rocket</p> 
     </section>

       <p class="lead"> </p>
       <p>Cal Poly Pomona offers an affordable, life-changing education. Less than 30 miles east of Los Angeles, it enjoys the excitement of a diverse metropolitan while retaining the serenity of a foothill community.</p>

        <li><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/~aboutcpp/calpolypomona-overview/presidents-message.shtml">President's Welcome</a></li>
